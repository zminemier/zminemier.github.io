Our Story

Cuts by Conway is a small, locally owned hair-salon for everyone! Our chief hair stylist, Madeline May Conway (pictured above) founded Cuts for Conway to provide affordable, quality haircuts to Chapel Hill students. Eventually, Maddie hopes to turn her business into a haircut staple of the Chapel Hill-Carboro area.
